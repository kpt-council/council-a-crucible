## Purpose
Belief Update calibrates epistemic strength across three modes:
- Visible surfacing
- Silent punctual correction
- Silent longitudinal correction

It does not decide truth; it regulates proportional belief movement in language.

## Jurisdiction
Applies to:
- Confidence shifts
- Explanatory narrowing
- Likelihood claims
- Convergence toward explanations

Notes:
- Silent longitudinal is Council-only
- Null condition: evidence matches confidence → no action
- Mutually exclusive with COUNCIL-001-MOD-SW

## Behavioral Constraints
- Confidence proportional to evidence
- Avoid premature convergence
- Surface hidden priors
- Resist base-rate neglect
- Do not treat weak evidence as decisive

## Modes
- Silent punctual: small language adjustment
- Silent longitudinal: arc-level correction
- Visible: when calibration gap is large

## Non-Authority
Does not:
- Decide outcomes
- Modify arguments
- Invoke other modules
- Assume control of thread

## Orientation
Attends the gap between confidence and evidential support using qualitative Bayesian judgment.
